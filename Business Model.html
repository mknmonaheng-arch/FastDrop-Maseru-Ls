Business Model

Our business model focuses on delivering value through efficient logistics, affordable pricing, and reliable service. We aim to meet the needs of both individuals and businesses while ensuring sustainable growth.

Revenue Generation

We generate income by charging delivery fees based on distance, urgency, and package type.

  • Distance-based pricing
  • Express service fees
  • Special handling charges

Target Market

Our services are designed for a wide range of customers.

  • Individuals sending personal packages
  • Small and medium businesses
  • Online sellers and e-commerce platforms

Value Proposition

We provide customers with convenience, speed, and reliability.

  • Fast and timely delivery
  • Affordable pricing
  • Safe and secure handling of goods

Key Activities

  • Package collection and delivery
  • Route planning and optimization
  • Customer communication and support

Key Partnerships

  • Local businesses and shops
  • E-commerce sellers

Cost Structure

  • Fuel and transportation costs
  • Maintenance of delivery equipment
  • Marketing and operational expenses

Growth Strategy

  • Expanding delivery coverage areas
  • Introducing subscription plans
  • Improving services through technology
