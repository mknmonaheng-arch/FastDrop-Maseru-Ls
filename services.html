Our Services

We provide a range of delivery solutions designed to meet your personal and business needs. Our services are fast, reliable, and tailored to ensure your packages arrive safely and on time.

Delivery Services

  • Standard Delivery: Affordable delivery within normal timeframes.
  • Express Delivery: Fast delivery for urgent packages.
  • Same-Day Delivery: Get your package delivered on the same day.

Business Solutions

  • E-commerce Delivery: Reliable delivery for online sellers (M200.00+).
  • Scheduled Deliveries: Plan deliveries in advance. (M200.00+)

Delivery Coverage

  • Mafeteng & Mohale's Hoek: Scheduled deliveries
